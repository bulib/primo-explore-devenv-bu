Welcome to BU Libraries Search

BU Libraries Search enables you to find books available in the BU Libraries, link to journals online, and discover scholarly articles for your research.

BU Libraries Search also helps you manage your research by formatting citations, or by exporting to citation managers such as Refworks, Zotero, and Mendeley.

Sign In to My Library Account to:

  • Save results in My Favorites
  • Request articles and books via Interlibrary Loan (ILL)
  • Renew books and other items on loan
  • Obtain additional article results
  • Save searches and set alerts for new results

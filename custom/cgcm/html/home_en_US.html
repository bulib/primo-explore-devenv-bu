Search the History of Missiology Collection

This searchable bibliography of digitized books provides access to classic writings primarily in the history of Protestant mission thought, including works on mission theology, theory, and strategy. Some of the books included here were digitized in a 2009 collaboration between Boston University's School of Theology Library, the Center for Global Christianity & Mission, and the Internet Archive, while others were digitized by other bodies and have been identified by BU STH scholars as relevant to the history of missiology in the context of the History of Missiology website. The Boston University School of Theology continues to work in collaboration with BU STH faculty to identify and digitize relevant works to enrich this collection.

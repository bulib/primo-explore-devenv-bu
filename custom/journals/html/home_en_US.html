BU Libraries Search - Journal and Newspaper Titles

This version of BU Libraries Search is limited to journal and newspaper titles that the BU Libraries subscribe to, in online and print formats.

To search for all library resources, please use the full version of BU Libraries Search at https://www.bu.edu/library/search

Search tips

In a Basic Search you'll receive results that contain all of your search terms. These may match keywords primarily in title or subject fields.

Use the Advanced Search instead to pre-limit to search only in specific fields, or to field titles that start with your query.

Use the Filters to limit to items available online or physically available in library.

The * character can be used as a wildcard in place of one or more characters. [Examples: recycl*, organiz*, hymn*]

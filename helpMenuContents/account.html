My Library Account

Loans

Books and other library items you have on loan will appear here. After signing in, you may renew selected or all items to extend the due date.

Loans can be saved to My Favorites, emailed, or exported to reference managers like RefWorks and Zotero.

Requests

Items already on loan to another patron can be requested for recall. Your requests will appear in this section.

**Note that Interlibrary Loan (ILL) requests and loans will not appear in My Library Account.

Fines

Refer to our Fines policies page for more details or questions about returned items still appearing in your account.

Services For Faculty & Instructors

NOTE: The Libraries will not be able to provide access to physical copies of books or other physical materials on Reserve during the fall semester. Faculty can request to have chapters of books scanned and placed on Reserve.

View our Course Materials Guide for more details on getting started with the Course Materials system.

Place books, articles, and other readings on electronic reserve for your students using the Place Items on Reserves form or the Faculty Scanning Requests form.

How to get it

BU Libraries can request materials on your behalf from other libraries. Search enables you to find many of these resources and place your request.

Articles

Millions of additional citations can be found in the More Articles Beyond BU scope.


These articles are identified as "Online Access unavailable. See Find in Library for print or request options".

Sign In to get a link to request this article through our InterLibrary Loan Service.


Books and other media

WorldCat contains records and holdings for other libraries. To use WorldCat directly in Search:

  • Select the drop down / scope for WorldCat and run your search
  • Once you Sign In, items not available at BU will have a link to "Request from Libraries Beyond BU"
  • After clicking this link, you'll go to a form to complete your interlibrary loan (ILL) request (if this is your first time requesting you'll be prompted to create an account)

Problems with your search results?

Sometimes problems arise with the search or bad data prevents you from finding it. We're here to help.

Email your question to ask@bu.edu. It is helpful to copy and include the URL of the page you're on in Search in your message.

Or start a chat using the menu in the lower right corner of the search results.

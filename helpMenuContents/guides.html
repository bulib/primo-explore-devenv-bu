About our Library Guides

We've created guides to help you get started with your research. You'll find these types in BU Libraries Search:

  • Research Guides: subject or topic oriented (suchas Philosophy or Contemporary African Politics)
  • Course Guides: for a specific course (such as a WR150 section or AD741)
  • How-to Guides: such as for creating bibliographies or finding newspapers

Finding Guides in Search

These are more commonly found in general keyword seaches. After searching, select the BU Research Guides filter under Material Types. You may also find links to Related Library Guides appearing in the full record display.

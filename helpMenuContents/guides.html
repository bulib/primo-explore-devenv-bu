About our Library Guides

We've created guides to help you get started with your research. You'll find these types in BU Libraries Search:

  • Research Guides: subject or topic oriented (such as Philosophy, Ethnomusicology, or Systems Engineering)
  • Course Guides: for a specific course (such as a WR150 section or SM323)
  • How To Guides: such as for creating bibliographies or finding newspapers

Finding Guides in Search

These are more commonly found in general keyword seaches. Look for results labeled Research Guides, or select the "BU Research Guides" filter under Material Types.


You may also find links to Related Library Guides appearing in the full record display.

You can search over guides explicitly by using the Library Help search drop down to pre-limit your search to guides.

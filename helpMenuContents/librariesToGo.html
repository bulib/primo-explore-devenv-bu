About BU Libraries 2 Go

The Boston University Libraries now offer a request and contactless pickup service for books and other circulating materials for research and study. Stacks at all Libraries locations are closed to patrons until further notice and materials can only be accessed digitally or via BU Libraries 2 Go.

How It Works

Select items are now available for request via BU Libraries 2 Go. Current BU faculty, students, and staff may request items.

Request through BU Libraries Search

  1. Find your book(s) in BU Libraries Search
  2. Sign in
  3. When items are available, the request button will appear
  4. Complete optional fields if necessary and click request to submit
  5. Youâ€™ll see immediate confirmation that your request was submitted
  6. You'll receive a notification to your BU email confirming your request with pickup details

Picking Up

Items will be ready for pickup the following day in the entrance foyer to Mugar Memorial Library. Pickup hours are from 9 am - 8 pm daily.

Please remember to wear your face covering, follow all directions on the signs near the pickup location, and observe distancing requirements.

Returning Materials

Items may be returned at the main BU Libraries drop box outside the GSU link near the eastern entrance.

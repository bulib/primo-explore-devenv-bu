Saved Searches

You can save searches to run again later, or to set alerts to be notifed via email when new records are available.

Saved search alerts are most effective when combined with filters, such as material type(s), date range, and avaialbility.

After doing a search...

  • Sign In first
  • Click the Save Search pin at the top of the results list.
  • You'll receive a prompt to optionally set a notifcation for email alerts
  • To return to your saved searches, click the pushpin icon in the upper right corner (same as for My Favorites)
  • Alerts can be enabled or disabled by selecting the bell icon

Search History

Your search history is saved during your session as long as keep the browser tab open. Sign In first to have your search history saved to your My Favorites account.

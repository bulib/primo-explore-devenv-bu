My Favorites

Favorites let you save results and label them for easy recall. To use:

  • Sign In first to save to your account
  • Click the pushpin icon next to the title to save to My Favorites
  • To view favorites, click the pushpin icon in the upper right corner
  • Organize your results by adding labels

Saved Searches

You can save searches to run again later or to set email alerts when new records are available.

  • After doing a search
  • Sign In first, click the Save Search pin at the top of the results list
  • You'll receive a prompt to optionally set a notifcation for email alerts
  • To return to your saved searches, click the pushpin icon in the upper right corner (same as for My Favorites)

Try Online First

Our building capacity has been significantly reduced to comply with public health guidelines. To avoid long waits, try connecting with us online before you come to our physical locations. We have expanded our online service delivery and may be able to help you without a visit to one of our branches.

For more information about connecting online, see our Using the Libraries Online overview or visit Ask a Librarian.

Who Can Visit

Current Boston University faculty, students, and staff.

Other groups should consult the university Policy Regarding Campus Visitors During the COVID-19 Pandemic.

When to Visit in Person

  • If you received an email that your BU Libraries 2 Go request or interlibrary loan materials are ready for pickup
  • Looking for a space to study? See available spaces and information about de-densification
  • Looking for a computers and printers? See available technology and changes to these services

What to Expect

  • Bring your BU ID card. All members of the BU community will be required to swipe their BU ID card to gain entry to Mugar Memorial Library.
  • Face coverings are required for entry and must be worn at all times in the building.
  • Building capacity has been significantly reduced and spaces have been reconfigured to maintain social distancing.
  • Lanes for queueing are marked outside the entrance to Mugar in the GSU Link.
  • In Mugar, all of your research needs our now located at a single Library Services Desk.
  • Follow signage in and around stairwells, restrooms, elevators, and study areas.
  • Refer to Back2BU for complete information on safety and guides on return to campus.

Material Types

  • Scholarly and Peer-Reviewed Articles
  • Books and eBooks
  • Journals
  • Databases
  • Dissertations
  • Music, Audio Recordings, and Scores
  • Films and Videos
  • Government Documents
  • Newspaper Articles
  • Reviews
  • Research and Course Guides
  • BU scholarship, manuscripts, and other unique collections from OpenBU

What am I searching?

BU Library Resources: by default you'll receive results that are available online or in one of our Libraries.

You can use drop downs or buttons to switch to searching:

  • More Articles Beyond BU: for articles available online or citations you may request.
  • WorldCat: books and other media held at other libraries.
  • Library Help contains curated subject, course and how-to guides for getting started and in-depth research needs.

See the Didn't Find It section for more details on requesting items not available at BU.

For some materials, you will need to access other resources or databases to find full content. This includes:

  • Articles in encyclopedias or dictionaries
  • Articles within a specific journal, newspaper or other periodical
  • Most statistical sources
  • Research Datasets

For these resources you may need to start your search in a more specific database. See our Databases A-Z list, or use Search and filter to material type "Databases".

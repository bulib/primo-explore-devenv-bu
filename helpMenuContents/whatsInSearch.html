Material Types

Search covers almost everything that BU Libraries owns or has access to, including subscription-only scholarly materials.
  • Scholarly and Peer-Reviewed Articles
  • Books and eBooks
  • Journals
  • Databases
  • Dissertations
  • Music, Audio Recordings, and Scores
  • Films and Videos
  • Government Documents
  • Newspaper Articles
  • Reviews
  • Research and Course Guides
  • BU scholarship, manuscripts, and other unique collections from OpenBU

What am I searching?

BU Library Resources: by default you'll receive results that are available online or in one of our Libraries. You can use drop downs or buttons to switch to searching: More Articles Beyond BU: for articles available online or citations you may request. WorldCat: books and other media held at other libraries. See Didn't Find It for more details on requesting items not available at BU.
